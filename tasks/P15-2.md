## interp.test.ts (harden the evaluator)

everywhere; bezier through 3+ irregularly spaced keys passes through every
key exactly; constant spans hold the LEFT value right up to (but not at) the
next key; mixed-interp curves (constant→linear→bezier spans) evaluate each
span by ITS left key's mode; fractional frames; equal-value keys stay flat
(no overshoot: bezier between equal values must be exactly that value —
Catmull-Rom guarantees it, prove it); large frame numbers.
