## Tangent frame (per hit)
The snapshot gives you triangle positions (triPos) and per-corner UVs (triUV).
At a hit on triangle i with barycentrics (u, v): compute the triangle's
tangent T from edge vectors e1=p1-p0, e2=p2-p0 and UV deltas
(du1,dv1)=(uv1-uv0), (du2,dv2)=(uv2-uv0): T = (e1*dv2 - e2*dv1)/det with
det=du1*dv2-du2*dv1; degenerate det (|det|<1e-12) or missing triUV → skip
perturbation (unperturbed N). Orthonormalize T against the geometric/shading
normal N, B = N×T. Normal map: n = sample*2-1, n.xy *= strength,
N' = normalize(T*n.x + B*n.y + N*max(n.z, 0.05)). Bump: sample the height at
(u±texel, v±texel) central differences like the GLSL, grad *= strength*4,
N' = normalize(N - T*grad.x - B*grad.y). Perturb the SHADING normal only
(keep the geometric normal for ray offsets / backface logic, matching how the
tracer already separates them — inspect before assuming).

## Rough/metal maps
At shade time: rough = clamp(rough * sample.r, 0.04, 1), metal *= sample.r
(sample the RED channel; the decoded pixels are RGB triplets). Only when the
respective image is non-null.
