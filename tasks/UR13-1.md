# UR13-1 — Cavity shading option (Blender-style ridge/valley)

## Goal
Blender's viewport Cavity: screen-space curvature accentuation — valleys
(concave creases) darken, ridges (convex edges) brighten. Reads surface detail
without AO's cost. New collapsible section in the shading dropdown (UR9-1
`makeSection` pattern), works in matcap/studio/rendered AND wireframe-with-
hidden-line (where the depth prime exists; classic see-through wireframe has
no surface — skip, document).

## shadePrefs (v5→v6 with migration)
`cavity: boolean` (default false), `cavityRidge: number` (0–2.5, default 1),
`cavityValley: number` (0–2.5, default 1), plus a `sections.cavity` disclosure
flag. Storage bump + migration chain (v3→…→v6 must still work).

## Implementation
- New pass or extension of the AO chain (`src/render/passes/aoPass.ts` owns
  the depth/normal prepass — REUSE that prepass; do not add a second one):
  screen-space curvature = divergence of view-space normals over a small
  pixel neighborhood (Blender's "screen" cavity): sample the 4-neighborhood
  normals from the prepass, curvature ≈ Σ dot(nᵢ−n₀, dirᵢ); positive →
  ridge, negative → valley. Output a factor texture (or fold into the AO
  factor path): `color · (1 − valley·k_v) · (1 + ridge·k_r)` applied where AO
  multiplies today (cavity works with AO on OR off — independent toggles;
  when both on they compose).
- Full resolution (it's cheap — one 5-tap pass), NOT the AO half-res chain
  (fine edges are the point). highp samplers (the lowp lesson).
- When cavity is on but AO off, the prepass must still run (gate currently
  keyed on shadePrefs.ao — generalize the gate).
- Sliders live-update (prefs read per frame).

## Acceptance criteria
- tsc clean; npm test green (prefs migration v5→v6 + full chain).
- e2e (extend e2e/shading.mjs — ALL existing checks stay green): (1) cavity
  on: a cube's top convex edge row is BRIGHTER than the adjacent face pixels
  and an interior crease (two cubes butted, or the donut's icing seam) is
  DARKER vs cavity off (region probes both directions); (2) ridge slider 0 →
  brightening gone, valley slider 0 → darkening gone; (3) AO+cavity together
  don't NaN/blow out (probe range); (4) wireframe hidden-line mode applies
  cavity (probe), see-through wireframe unaffected; (5) disclosure section
  persists.
- Green on SwiftShader AND E2E_GPU=1 (screen-space derivative work — exactly
  the driver-sensitive class). Orbit-sweep contact sheet on the donut fixture

  from single views).
- STRICT scope: shadePrefs, shadingMenu, aoPass (or new cavityPass),
